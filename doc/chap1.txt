  finitely  generated  abelian  group  [23XA[123X  it  represents  is equivalent to the
  cokernel  of  [23Xf:\mathbb{Z}^n\to  \mathbb{Z}^m[123X.  The readers who are familiar
  with  the  homological  algebra  could  think [23Xf[123X as the free resolutoin of [23XA[123X.
  Actually,  almost  all  algorithms  in  this package are developed using the
  knowledge  of  homological algebra. For example, we use the mapping cone and
  cocone to compute kernel and cokernel. For more details, see the appendix of
